Who We Are

Haji Wadud CNG & Pick-up Parts is Comilla's leading supplier of genuine automotive components — serving mechanics, dealers, and vehicle owners across Bangladesh since 2003.

We specialize in high-quality CNG three-wheeler and pick-up vehicle parts, stocking over 500 different components from the most trusted brands in the industry. Our inventory spans engine components, electrical systems, bearings, tires, lubricants, and much more — all sourced directly from authorized manufacturers.

Over two decades of operation in Comilla, Chittagong have given us an intimate understanding of the local automotive market. We know what mechanics need, what vehicle owners demand, and how to deliver it at prices that make real business sense.

"Our commitment is simple — every part we sell must be genuine, every price must be fair, and every customer must leave satisfied. That promise has been the foundation of Haji Wadud Parts since the day we opened."

Our History

From a small parts shop in Comilla to one of the region's most trusted automotive suppliers — here's how we grew.

2003

Founded in Comilla

Haji Wadud Parts opened its doors as a small CNG spare parts retailer, serving local mechanics and vehicle owners in the Comilla area.

2007

First Wholesale Partnership

Established our first direct wholesale agreement with BAJAJ-authorized distributors, enabling us to offer prices unavailable elsewhere in the region.

2012

Expanded to Pick-up Parts

Growing demand from the pick-up truck segment led us to significantly expand our catalog, adding VIRA, MAT, and VARROC product lines.

2018

500+ Products Milestone

Our inventory surpassed 500 unique SKUs, cementing our position as the most comprehensive CNG & pick-up parts supplier in Chittagong division.

2024

Online Catalog & WhatsApp Orders

Launched our digital presence with a full online product catalog and WhatsApp ordering — making it easier than ever for dealers and mechanics to order from anywhere.
